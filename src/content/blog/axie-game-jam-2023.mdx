Sky Mavis is organizing the [Axie Game Jam 2023](https://hub.skymavis.com/axie-game-jam/2023)! A game making marathon where indie game developers or teams come together to make an Axie game that fits a theme, in just 2 weeks.

Participating in game jams is always a blast!

This is an good chance to dive into Colyseus and, even better, stand a chance to win some prizes!

Don't miss this demo using Colyseus and Cocos Creator, thoughtfully crafted by the game jam organizers to give you a head start in creating your multiplayer game.
